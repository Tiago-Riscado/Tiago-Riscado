### 🧬 About Me

🎓 Final-year **BSc in AI & Data Science**  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;@ **Universidade da Beira Interior**, Portugal

🔬 Thesis on **zero-shot medical image classification**  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;using **MedImageInsight** & **CLIP** on the **ISIC2018** dataset

🧠 Deep interest in **Vision-Language Models**  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;— contrastive learning, embedding-based inference, UniCL

💬 Also working on **NLP** — zero-shot emotion  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;classification with **LLaMA** & **Mistral** via OpenRouter

✍️ I care about clean architecture, modular pipelines
